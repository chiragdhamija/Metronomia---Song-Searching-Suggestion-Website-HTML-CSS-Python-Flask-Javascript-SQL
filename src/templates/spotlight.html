In The Limelight


Nucleya is a highly respected and accomplished Indian electronic music producer and DJ. He is widely regarded as a pioneer in the Indian electronic music scene and has made a significant impact with his unique and innovative approach to blending traditional Indian music with electronic dance music. His music is a true reflection of his artistic vision, and he has gained a loyal following of fans who appreciate his ability to create exciting and memorable beats. Nucleya has collaborated with some of the most well-known artists in the industry and has performed at some of the most prestigious music festivals around the world. His contribution to the electronic music scene in India is immeasurable, and he is a true inspiration to aspiring musicians everywhere.
